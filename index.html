Overview

CO2 Renew is a professional-grade carboxytherapy treatment that begins working immediately upon application. It interacts with water within the skin to create a concentrated CO2 reservoir beneath the surface.

This controlled increase in carbon dioxide stimulates enhanced oxygen delivery through the Bohr effect—supporting circulation, cellular energy, and the skin’s natural regenerative functions.

The result is visibly brighter, smoother, calmer, and more resilient-looking skin.
